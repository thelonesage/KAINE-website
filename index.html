WHAT WE FIND

The findings that sit invisible in your data — until we look.

These are not hypothetical insights. These are the exact types of findings we surface for e-commerce businesses every time we run a diagnostic.

Ad spend flowing to the wrong audience

Your highest-converting customers look nothing like the audience your campaigns are targeting — and the gap is costing you every day.

A product doing the work of five

One SKU is quietly generating the majority of your revenue while receiving the least marketing attention of any product in your store.

Customers who buy once and vanish

Your repeat purchase rate is low not because of your product — because of what happens, or doesn't happen, in the 72 hours after the first order.

Margin leaking through your pricing

Your most promoted products are your lowest-margin SKUs. You are spending money to sell the things that make you the least.

Revenue that looks consistent but isn't

Month-on-month numbers look stable on the surface. Underneath, your customer base is churning and new acquisition is masking the loss.

Inventory decisions made by memory

Your bestsellers run out during peak periods while slow-moving stock ties up cash. The pattern is predictable — but only if someone is looking at the data.
