ABOUT KAINE.io

Built to solve the problem I kept watching happen.

Every week in Nigeria, e-commerce founders make pricing, inventory, and marketing decisions by gut feel, not because they don't care about data, but because nothing accessible, intelligent, and affordable existed to translate their data into decisions. KAINE.io exists to change that.

THE FOUNDER

Clinton Ofoedu

Founder, KAINE.io · Benin City, Nigeria

I started building KAINE.io because I couldn't stop noticing the gap. E-commerce founders in Nigeria were running real businesses, generating revenue, managing inventory, spending on ads, and making almost every major decision without a single piece of analytical evidence to support it.

Not because they didn't want to use data. But because the tools that exist are either too expensive, too technical, or not built for the reality of running a Nigerian e-commerce business in 2026. The platforms are different. The payment infrastructure is different. The market dynamics are different. And the available help was built for someone else.

KAINE.io is what I would have wanted to exist. A firm that takes your data, whatever state it's in, and tells you what it actually says. In plain language. With specific recommendations. Without requiring you to understand a database.

"A freelancer solves the task. A consultant solves the problem behind the task. That distinction is the entire philosophy of KAINE.io."

Clinton Ofoedu

Founder, KAINE.io
