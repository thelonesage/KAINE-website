HOW WE WORK

Structured. Evidence-backed. Delivered for founders, not data scientists.

Every KAINE.io engagement follows the same rigorous process — regardless of service level. This is how we work.

01

You submit the diagnostic form.

You fill in our structured intake form — your business, your channels, your tools, and the problem you're trying to solve. Takes 3 minutes. This is where we start.

02

We review your submission personally.

No automation. No chatbot. A human reads your submission within 24 hours and sends you an initial read on your situation — what we see and what we'd need to dig into.

03

We connect to your data.

You share your transaction records, ad performance data, order history — whatever you have. Messy is fine. Incomplete is fine. We have cleaned worse. We sign a confidentiality agreement before anything is shared.

04

We run the analysis.

Our AI-augmented analytical pipeline cleans your data, detects patterns, runs diagnostic queries across your customer, product, and marketing data, and surfaces findings ranked by business impact. The human layer then interprets, contextualises, and translates.

05

You receive your deliverable.

A structured report written for a founder — not a data scientist. Executive summary, key findings, and specific recommendations you can act on immediately. For retainer clients, this repeats every month.
