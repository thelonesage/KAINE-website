DATA ANALYTICS

We extract the signal from your data. Then we show you what it means.

Most e-commerce businesses in Nigeria are data-rich and insight-poor. The records exist — in Paystack, in WhatsApp order threads, in Instagram DMs, in Bumpa reports. What's missing is the analytical layer that connects the dots. That is what we build.

Data Cleaning & Structuring

We take your raw, messy, fragmented records and turn them into clean, analysis-ready datasets. Duplicate transactions, inconsistent product names, missing fields — all of it resolved before a single insight is drawn.

Descriptive & Diagnostic Analytics

We tell you what happened — and why. Revenue trends, customer behaviour patterns, product performance, channel attribution. Every finding is tied to a specific business question you need answered.

Custom Dashboard Delivery

For retainer clients, we build and maintain a live dashboard that gives you real-time visibility into your key metrics — without you needing to know how to read a database.

BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE

The difference between a report and an insight is what you do with it.

Business intelligence is not just about having data — it's about having the right data in front of the right person at the right moment. We build the intelligence layer that connects your data to your decisions.

Revenue & Margin Intelligence

We map exactly where your revenue comes from, where it leaks, and which parts of your business are generating the most and least margin per naira of effort.

Customer Intelligence

We build a complete picture of your customer base — who your best customers are, what they have in common, how to find more of them, and how to stop losing the ones you already have.

Marketing Attribution Intelligence

We tell you which naira of marketing spend is producing buyers and which is producing nothing. Then we show you how to reallocate.
