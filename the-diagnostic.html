THE PROBLEM

Most e-commerce businesses in Nigeria are running on instinct. The data exists. Nobody is reading it.

Your revenue is inconsistent and you don't know why

Some months are strong. Others collapse. The pattern is in your data, but only if someone is actually looking at it.

Your ad spend is a black box

You see reach and impressions. You don't see which naira produced a paying customer and which naira disappeared.

Your pricing decisions are based on feel

You set prices and run promotions based on instinct, competitor watching, or WhatsApp group advice. The margin impact is invisible until it isn't.

You have data but no intelligence

Paystack exports. Bumpa reports. Spreadsheets from someone on the team. All of it exists. None of it is connected into an answer.

WHAT YOU RECEIVE

A structured diagnostic report, written for a founder, not a data scientist.

Executive Summary

A one-page read of the most important things we found, written so you can brief your team or make a decision within 5 minutes of opening the document.

Key Findings

Every significant pattern we detected in your data, presented clearly, with the business implication of each finding explained in plain language.

Ranked Recommendations

Specific actions, ranked by expected business impact. Not a list of things to consider, a prioritised plan of what to do first, second, and third.

A 30-Minute Walkthrough Call

After you receive the report, we schedule a call to walk you through the findings, answer your questions, and ensure you know exactly how to act on every recommendation.
